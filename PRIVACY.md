# Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-03-03

Temposolo Auto-Stop does not collect, store, or transmit personal data.

## What the extension does
The extension runs locally in your browser and only operates on the Temposolo timer page (`https://temposolo.es/timer*`) to stop a running timer when your computer is locked or when the browser is suspending.

## Data access and processing
- The extension does not send data to any external server.
- The extension does not use analytics or tracking.
- The content script may read the Temposolo timer state from the page's localStorage to determine whether the timer is running.
- The content script may click the Stop button on the page to stop the timer. If the button is not found, it may update the same localStorage entry on the page to stop the timer.

## Data sharing
The extension does not sell, share, or transfer user data to third parties.

## Contact
If you have questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue in the project repository.
